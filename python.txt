LEGB Rule -----------The LEGB rule explains how python searchs for varible in different scopes.
L --- local.
------------------variables inside the current function.

-----------------variables in the outer function(if there is a nested function) 
G --- Global 
----------------variables defined at the top level of program.
B --- Build-In
---------------Python predefined name and function (like print, len, etc)
